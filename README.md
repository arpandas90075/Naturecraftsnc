# 🌿 NatureCrafts — Handmade Soap Store

**Devoted to Nature.**

NatureCrafts is an online store for handmade, small-batch soaps. Every bar is poured with cold-pressed oils and botanical ingredients, cured on wooden racks for weeks, then cut, stamped and wrapped by hand in paper and twine — no factories, no plastic, no detergents.

🛒 **Visit the store:** https://naturecraftsnc.netlify.app

---

## 🧼 How to shop

**1. Browse the collection.**
Open the **Shop** page to see every bar — Wild Honey & Oat, Charcoal & Cedar, Neem & Tulsi and more. Tap any soap (or its **Details** button) to read its full story, ingredients and price.

**2. Fill your cart.**
Hit **Add to cart** on any soap. The button turns into a **− / +** counter so you can adjust the quantity right there, and the cart icon at the top keeps count. Open the **Cart** page any time to review — you'll see the subtotal, delivery charge, and **your exact total before you commit to anything**.

**3. Check out.**
Press **Proceed to checkout** and fill in your delivery details — name, email, mobile number, house/flat, full address and pincode.

> 📍 We currently deliver **within Delhi only** (pincodes starting with 110).

**4. Pay when it arrives.**
There is no online payment. Every order is automatically set to **UPI or cash on arrival** — you pay only when the parcel is in your hands.

**5. Get your e-bill instantly.**
The moment you place the order, a styled e-bill lands in your email with your **Order ID**, the itemized total, and your **expected delivery date** (one week from ordering).

**6. Track your order.**
On the **Track Order** page, enter your Order ID from the e-bill. You'll see a live timeline —

`Placed ✓ ──── In Delivery ──── Delivered`

— along with the expected date, which updates as your soap makes its way to you.

## 📖 The story

NatureCrafts began with a simple frustration: soap had become a factory product — hardened detergents in plastic, printed to look natural. We went back to the slow way. No two of our bars are perfectly identical. That's the point.
